District Profile

The District is located in the central part of the Northern Region. It shares boundaries with Saveligu Municipal to the North and Sagnarigu and Tamale Metro to the south. To the west, Nanton District shares boundary with the Kumbungu District while the Karaga District is located at the East of the District. Also, the Mion District shares boundary at the southern part of the Nanton District. The District has a total land mass of about 1790.70sq km.

Population Distribution

The population of Nanton District is according to the (2010PHC) 77,706 at a growth rate of 2.7% with 167 communities, 2 area councils, 118, unit committee, 20 electoral area and a constituency


Potentials of the District

Basic Governance and Leadership Structure

The Nanton District Assembly has a total membership of twenty-seven (27), consisting of twenty (20) elected, seven (7) government appointees, one (1) Member of Parliament and one (1) District Chief Executive out of which the female membership stands at two (2). There are twenty (20) Electoral Areas and hundred and eighteen (118) Unit Committees, and two (2) Area Councils; these are Nanton Area Council and the Tampion Area Council. The District has a working local authority which is the Nanton District Assembly, headed by the District Chief Executive with the District Co-ordinating Director as the Chief Technocrat who has the responsibility of co-ordinating the activities of the various departments.
