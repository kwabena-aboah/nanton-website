  • Vision

    The Nanton District Assembly is envisioned too become a model Local Government Authority that delivers quality services to meet the needs and aspirations of its citizens especially vulnerable groups and those that are excluded in society.

  • Mission

    The Nanton District Assembly exists to improve upon the living standards of citizens of the district through effective mobilization and efficient application of resources to implement development initiatives with the active participation of beneficiary institutions and communities to create opportunities for all.

  • Core values

    The principles the management and staff of the Nanton District Assembly uphold and apply to guide their conduct and perform their duties with efficiency, effectively and with mutual respect for all stakeholders
